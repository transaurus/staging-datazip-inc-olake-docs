Program Overview

Empowering contributors to shape the future of data engineering

What is the Contributor Program?

The OLake Contributor Program is a community collaboration initiative designed to improve the quality and expand the ecosystem of OLake connectors. We sponsor various tasks around features, usability, and reliability while empowering both veteran contributors and newcomers to make meaningful contributions to the project.

How it Works

Getting started is simple. After reviewing our contributing guide, explore the good first issues and Join our Slack community:

  • Get direct support from maintainers
  • Collaborate with other contributors
  • Access exclusive resources and documentation
  • Participate in contributor-only events
  • Track your contributions and rewards
